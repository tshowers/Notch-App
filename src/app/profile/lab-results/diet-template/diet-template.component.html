Attached is a sample rotation diet guideline based on your personal test results. The concept behind rotation-style elimination diets is to encourage nutritional diversification while eliminating the most reactive foods and rotating in foods that were lower on the reactivity scale.

Our rotation diet guideline is designed so that you don’t consume the same food more than once every four days and no foods of the same family more than once every two days. The guideline includes all foods that received a low score on the reactivity scale. The rotation diet plan alternates the food families based on a four-day cycle, ensuring that no food is consumed more than once every four days.

Please note that this guideline isn’t mandatory – it is only meant to serve as a tool for those looking to pursue a rotation-style elimination diet. Notch Health provides this diet guideline for your consideration, but your results are best interpreted by a healthcare practitioner who can provide personalized guidance and recommend specific dietary changes.
